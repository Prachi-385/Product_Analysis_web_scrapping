# Project Title

E-commerce Product Analysis using Web Scraping & Data Analytics

# Overview

This project focuses on extracting product data from an e-commerce-style website and performing data analysis to uncover insights related to pricing strategy, customer satisfaction, and product performance.

🎯 Objectives
- Analyze relationship between price and customer ratings
- Identify high-performing and low-performing products
- Detect pricing inefficiencies
- Provide actionable business recommendations

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- BeautifulSoup (Web Scraping)
- Matplotlib (Data Visualization)
- Jupyter Notebook

# Key Analysis Performed
- Price Distribution Analysis
- Rating Distribution Analysis
- Price vs Rating Relationship
- Best vs Worst Value Products
- Feature Engineering (Price Category, Rating Category)

🔍 Key Insights
- Majority of products have low to average ratings, indicating quality gaps
- No strong correlation between price and rating
- Presence of high-rated low-price products (value opportunities)
- Overpriced low-rated products highlight pricing inefficiencies

💼 Business Recommendations
- Implement value-based pricing strategy
- Improve or remove low-rated products
- Promote high-rated affordable products
- Introduce quality control benchmarks

📈 Business Impact
- Improved customer satisfaction
- Increased conversion rates
- Better pricing optimization
- Stronger product positioning
